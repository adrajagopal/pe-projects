Our story

We - Aditya and Shriya, cousins, best friends - grew up playing in the kitchen of our parents' kitchen. Our parents opened Chennai House, a local institution, over 30 years ago.

Over the years, as we grew up, we developed a mastery of South Indian cuisine, but also took interest in fashion, art, and music. Then, on a trip to France after high school, we discovered the joys of haute French cuisine. What started as a brief vacation, turned into a 2-year stint working in the top kitchens in Paris.

Fennelope is a blend of the two cultures - traditional South Indian cuisine and elevated French dining. We hope you join us for the journey. Our food truck is just the start. Our goal is to spread the joy of elevated South Indian food and open a brick-and-mortar location.
